about us

    Why does learning to code have to be so grueling?

     It doesn’t.


    That’s why Mindspark exists. We were created in an attempt to make learning to code more fun. These days, in coding bootcamps, students are bogged down by hours and hours worth of lectures on programming concepts and syntax. And it’s not even that effective.

    Here at Mindspark, we’ve dedicated ourselves to creating a much more hands-on curriculum. Each course is carefully crafted to perfection to ensure the students are getting out of the program as much as they possibly can, with a great balance of fun!

    We are reusing a working formula and making it better. Mindspark’s 2015 students loved the old curriculum, and when anonymously surveyed, they rated the program an average of 9/10. This year, we’re taking this formula that we know already works, and perfecting it. We can guarantee your child will have a great time at our summer program while, at the same time, learning a lot of programming.
